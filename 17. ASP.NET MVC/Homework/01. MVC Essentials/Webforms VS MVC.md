# Webforms vs MVC

## Similarities
* They both use the ASP.NET Framework
* They have the same Application Life Cycle 

## Differences
* Different standart view engines.
* MVC follows the Model–view–controller architectural pattern, while Webfors aims to simulate the standart architecture of a windows app.
* Webforms has ready to use controls (widgets) that offer alot of functionality.
* MVC has better separation of concerns, is more testable and gives you full control over what reaches to client.
